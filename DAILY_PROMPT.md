# Daily prompt

> Copy and paste this into your AI tool each morning (Claude conversation, ChatGPT chat, Gemini, etc.). Fill in the brackets with what you want the agent to look at today.

---

```
Run today's digest.

Read beat-notes.md, source-list.md, and output-format.md (loaded in your context) first.

Today, focus on: [TOPIC OR THREAD — e.g., "GP contract negotiations" or "anything new on weight-loss drug rollout"]

Sources to check today:
- [URL or feed 1]
- [URL or feed 2]
- [URL or feed 3]

Time window: [e.g., "last 24 hours" or "since Monday"]

Anything specific to watch for: [optional — e.g., "any quotes from named DHSC sources" or "leaked drafts"]

Produce the digest in the format specified in output-format.md.
```

---

## Tips

- **The first few digests will be rough.** That's expected. Each one teaches you what to add to your context files. After a week or two, you'll have shaped the agent into something genuinely yours.

- **Update beat-notes.md weekly.** "What's hot right now" is the highest-signal section — it should change as your reporting changes.

- **If the digest surfaces something useful, add the source to source-list.md.** If it surfaces noise from a source, add that source to Tier 3.

- **If a digest feels off, ask the agent why it ranked items the way it did.** It will tell you, and you'll learn what to adjust in the context files.
